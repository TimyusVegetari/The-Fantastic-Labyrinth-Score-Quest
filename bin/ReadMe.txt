 Keyboard Shortcuts: Title screen
   Top and bottom arrows: Select buttons in the menu
   Enter:                 Activate the selected button
   
 Keyboard Shortcuts: Title screen
   Top, right, etc... arrows: Move your character
   C:                         Collect a food on the floor
   I:                         Open/Close the inventory at the top of screen
   Top and bottom arrows:     Select a food
   Enter:                     Eat the selected food
 
 Goal of the game:
   You have go out of every level with enough score to hope to exceed you own
   best score. When you move in the labyrinth, your current score decrease and
   if your score reaches zero, you will die. To help you, a little food is
   spread in the labyrinth.
   Good luke :)
